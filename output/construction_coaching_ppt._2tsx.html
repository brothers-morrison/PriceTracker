Build Better Teams: Coaching and Team Development for Construction Managers

A 120-Minute Workshop for Middle Managers

Boise State University PACE

Learning Approach

Mixed teaching: micro-lectures, discussions, templates
Construction-focused scenarios
Practical, take-home tools

The Cost of Miscommunication

Small miscommunications cascade into:

Costly delays
Expensive rework
Safety risks

"What's one coaching moment you wish you could have handled differently?"

Why This Matters

Teams that practice structured coaching report higher reliability and fewer rework events

Activity: Think-Pair-Share

2 minutes: Reflect individually, then share

Learning Outcomes

  1. Conduct structured coaching using a 4-step model
  2. Lead 15-minute daily team huddles
  3. Map team roles and identify misalignments
  4. Create a 90-day action plan

Block 4: Psychological Safety (10 min)

Psychological Safety

Definition

A climate where people feel safe to take risks and speak up

3 Practical Cues

1. PAUSE before deciding
2. INVITE input
3. APPRECIATE contributions

Activity: Micro-Trust Exercise

Practice Inclusive Behaviors

"I'll pause and ask for your input"

"What concerns do you have?"

"Thank you for raising that"
